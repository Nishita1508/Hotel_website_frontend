Welcome to Saffron Sunset

Architecturally inspired by the stepwells of India and the traditional ‘toran’ gateways adorning its façade, Saffron Sunset welcomes you with regal splendour. In keeping with the philosophy of Saffron Sunset Hotels, Saffron Sunset is a glowing celebration of the culture, arts and crafts of the region. A prime location and luxurious facilities make each stay memorable.

In line with Saffron Sunset Hotels’ philosophy of being rooted to the soil, Saffron Sunset draws inspiration from the river along with the iconic architecture, vibrant culture and grandeur of Gujarat.
